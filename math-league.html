Everything about Math League

‘Budhanilkantha Math League 2022’ is a Nationwide Mathematics Olympiad in Nepal with students from classes 11 and 12/ A-levels/ IB diploma from various schools and colleges across the country contending. The league is a mathematics Olympiad that introduces an international competition format (based on International Mathematics Competition standards) at the national level. The league serves as a platform for students to showcase their problem-solving abilities through skill-based mathematical challenges highly relatable with our daily lives. The League also aims to provide an opportunity for students to exchange various mathematical ideas, problem- solving approaches and engage in cultural and social experiences in the variety of participating schools


This is not just a mathematics competition; it is a platform where you realise your intellect in math solving the most logical, practical and baffling problems individually and in groups.


Budhanilkantha Math League is a three-day seminar for students pursuing their +2, A-levels, IB diploma or the CBSE boards. You don’t need to be a genius. You just need to know simple algebra and geometry and you’ll be fine. We have the most amazing prizes to offer in competitions that are even out of mathematics.
